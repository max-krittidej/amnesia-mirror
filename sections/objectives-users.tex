  \item \field{Project Objectives}

    \begin{enumerate}
      \item Support independent daily living with ambient medication, orientation, and
        task prompts that require no phone, app, or wearable.
      \item Extend the same prompt engine from home routines to work-related task
        sequences, supporting living, learning, and earning.
      \item Reduce daily supervision demand on family caregivers and job coaches.
      \item Deliver an affordable, non-stigmatising, privacy-by-design device suitable
        for Thai homes and later supported workplaces.
    \end{enumerate}

  \item \field{Target Users}

    \textbf{Primary users.} Working-age adults with cognitive or memory disability who
    live independently or semi-independently and want to build autonomy and, where
    possible, employment.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \textbf{Disability type / accessibility need:} intellectual disability;
        traumatic brain injury; post-stroke cognitive impairment; early-onset
        dementia. The shared, addressable need is impairment of memory, attention,
        sequencing, and follow-through.
      \item \textbf{Age range:} working-age adults, 15--59.
      \item \textbf{Usage context:} the home, positioned at a fixed point in the
        daily routine (bathroom or hallway); designed to extend into supported
        employment settings in a later phase.
      \item \textbf{Estimated number of target users:} intellectual plus mental or
        behavioural disability accounts for about 312{,}000 registered people, within
        Thailand's roughly 855{,}000 working-age people with disability~[2, 3].
      \item \textbf{Secondary users:} family caregivers (who benefit from reduced
        supervision load) and, prospectively, job coaches and supported-employment
        providers.
      \item \textbf{Related organisations / communities:} DEP, disability foundations,
        vocational-rehabilitation centres, community health services, and employers
        participating in the disability employment quota.
    \end{itemize}
